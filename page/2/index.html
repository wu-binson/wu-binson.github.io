Notion In The Hundred-Page Machine Learning Book

发表于 2020-03-10 | 更新于: 2020-03-10 | 分类于 数学 | 阅读次数:
  1. A vector is denoted as bold lowercase letter, such as x or w
  2. $x^{(j)}$: the index $j$ denotes specific dimension of the vector.
  3. Matrices are denoted with bold capital letters, such as A, W.
  4. A Set is denoted as a calligraphic capital letters, such as $\mathcal{S}$
  5. A random variable, usually written as an italic capital letter, like $\textit{X}$
  6. The Expectation of $\textit X$ denoted as $\mathbb{E}[\textit X]=\mu$.
  7. Standard deviation: $\sigma = \sqrt{\mathbb E[{(\textit X-\mu)}^2]}$
  8. Variance: $\sigma^2$ or $var(\textit X)$
  9. Sample Standard Deviation: $\mathcal s$
  10. If $\mathbb E[\hat \theta(S_X)]=\theta$, where $\hat \theta$ Is a sample statistic, $\hat \theta$ is called unbiased Estimator.
